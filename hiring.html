AI Engineer (NLP)

We are looking for an NLP Engineer to work with us on cutting-edge problems in language processing. The ideal candidate should be:

  • Capable of building end-to-end, deployable NLP solutions, preferably in Python (should be able to adapt to other programming languages as needed)
  • Familiar with standard NLP toolkits (Spacy, NLTK, etc.), pipelines (morphological analysis, POS tagging, parsing), and data curation/annotation requirements
  • Enthusiastic about analyzing text data and understanding linguistic features specific to the domain
  • Fluent with modern deep learning architectures (CNNs, LSTMs, Transformers etc.) and Tensorflow/Pytorch/Keras
  • Capable of adapting and implementing techniques from the research literature
  • (Optional) Conversant with cloud frameworks such as GCP, Azure & Sagemaker

If you are a competent software developer looking to expand your experience and skills in the area of NLP/AI, Inscripta provides you an exciting opportunity to:

  • Challenge yourself with highly relevant and well-formulated business problems in language technology, focusing intensely on the technical quality of solutions
  • Apply cutting-edge techniques in NLP, ML, and Deep Learning
  • Work with experienced NLP/ML practitioners on a variety of problems in a fast-paced, informal, startup environment
  • Gain exposure to delivering NLP-centric real-world solutions

Location: Bangalore/Mumbai/Remote

Experience: 2+ years of NLP experience would be desirable, but freshers with exceptional NLP skills can be considered too.
